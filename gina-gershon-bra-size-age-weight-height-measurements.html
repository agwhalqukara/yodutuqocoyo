    Biography:

    Gina Gershon was born on June 10, 1962 in Los Angeles, California, USA. She attended New York University where she completed a BFA in drama and psychology.

    She then studied at the Circle in the Square Professional Theater School in New York where she worked David Mamet, Harold Guskin and Sandra Seacat.

    She made her film debut in Pretty in Pink. She has since appeared in other films including Borderline, P.S. I Love You, Dreamland, and Killer Joe.

    She also became known for her television roles as Glenn Hall in Snoops, Catwoman / Selina Kyle in The Batman and as Valerie in Rescue Me.
