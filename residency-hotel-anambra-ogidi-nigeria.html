    Leophine Residency Hotel Anambra is budget hotel combining functionality and style to cater to the needs of business or leisure travellers at Km 13 Old Enugu-Onitsha road, opposite Ogbunike Boys’ secondary school, Ogidi, Anambra, Nigeria. This hotel is a remodelled version, with a modern twist, of the first hotel that was built in 1980.

    This hotel with 52 rooms grouped into Residency Deluxe, Residency Executive Deluxe, Executive, Residency Executive Suite and Presidential Suite is designed to suit even the most selective of guests The rooms at this hotel are spacious and furnished with custom-made furniture pieces. The rooms also come with High Definition flat screen televisions that are equipped with 15 premium DSTV channels and regular channels, a telephone, free internet access, wood-top business desk and executive chair, a mini bar (only on request) and free bottled water.

    Leophine Residency Hotel Anambra offers a variety of dishes and alcoholic & non-alcoholic drinks from the on-site restaurant and bar respectively. This hotel in Anambra features a gymnasium, a squash court, an outdoor swimming pool, four event spaces (including the 1000-seater Onyemaechi auditorium hall), a 2000-square meter field for entertainment (featuring live bands and a DJ), laundry services and airport shuttle services (with the option of armed guards).
