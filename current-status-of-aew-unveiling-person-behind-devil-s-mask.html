    Current Status of AEW Unveiling Person Behind Devils Mask

    MJF has been involved in a major storyline featuring a wrestler wearing his Devil mask, as the latter has been stalking and harassing him for the past two months. Now the current status for AEW unveiling the person behind the Devils mask has been revealed.

    23 Sep 2024, 00:00

    MJF has been involved in a major storyline featuring a wrestler wearing his Devil mask, as the latter has been stalking and harassing him for the past two months. Now the current status for AEW unveiling the person behind the Devil’s mask has been revealed.

    This week’s episode of AEW Dynamite was night full of solid in-ring action and memorable moments. This included major update on MJF’s feud with the person posing as The Devil, which mostly saw MJF being knocked unconscious backstage by what fans assume was The Devil.

    According to Sean Ross Sapp behind Fightful’s paywall, it was reported that AEW has not made any changes to the person behind the Devil’s mask nor any changes to the other members either.

    ” It was reiterated to us that as of last week, the talent that are slated to play the “devils” on AEW TV were still being portrayed by those under the mask. “

    It remains to be seen who will end up being revealed as the man behind The Devil’s Mask. With Jack Perry no longer being under suspension, fans believe it might just be him.
