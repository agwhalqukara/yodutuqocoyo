    Daily Bumps is a popular American YouTube channel. The channel is run by Bryan Lanning and Missy Lanning, and they post vlogs on their channel. They have two sons, Oliver James Ollie Lanning and Finley Jay Finn Lanning, and they appear in their vlogs as well. The vlogs are based on the challenges of raising a family and the happiness & lessons that come along with it.

    Inception

    The channel was started on the 31st of December 2012, while the first video was uploaded on the 26th of January 2013. The channel was initially started to document Missy’s pregnancy journey and to help mothers with stillbirths. The first uploaded video uploaded to the channel, which was a daily vlog, has gained 955K views as of May 2020.

    Evolution

    Their vlogs document several important events in their life, such as their kid learning to walk and their kids’ birthday parties. Their more popular videos include the “Toy Story Halloween Special,” “Baby Trapped In Waterpark,” and “The Baby Is Coming,” which has gained 136 Million views, 50 Million views, and 22 Million views respectively as of May 2020.
