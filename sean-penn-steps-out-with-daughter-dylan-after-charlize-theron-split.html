    Family time after breakup. On Sunday, June 21, Sean Penn was spotted out and about in Los Angeles with his daughter, Dylan Penn. It was his first photographed outing since Us Weekly broke the news that Charlize Theron had ended her engagement to the fellow Oscar winner last week. 

    Penn, 54, was dressed casually in a T-shirt, jeans, and leather jacket as was his model daughter, 24, who wore a sweatshirt and jeans to go on a walk with her dad on Father’s Day. (The celebrity humanitarian shares Dylan and her brother, Hopper, 21, with his second ex-wife Robin Wright.)

    As Us previously reported, Theron, 39, abruptly stopped communicating with her former fiancé following the Cannes Film Festival in May.
